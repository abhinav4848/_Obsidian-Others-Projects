# Timeful text
[Mass mediums mostly lack an authored time dimension beyond a day](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zBcm1RNbzedjDMTt8rRxoWt). This seems like a powerful authorial dimension to explore, as illustrated by e.g. [[Guided meditation apps are naturally spread over time]].

We might call texts with an authored time dimension _timeful texts._
- [[The mnemonic medium keeps readers in contact with material over time]]
- [The mnemonic medium can be adapted to author an experience which unfolds over time](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zKRrv2DmKp3VyDEp3oXrKXQ)
    - [Mnemonic texts could be serialized in small sections over time, timed to reader recall](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zJaSxZHpWr5fq7vQtHUVuMp) (like [Execute Program’s lessons don’t unlock until you’ve successfully reviewed their prerequisites](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/zHLWyC5LVXZLWE9RmoC1gua))

Some examples:
- [The Artist’s Way - Julia Cameron](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/The_Artist%E2%80%99s_Way_-_Julia_Cameron)
- [Guided meditation app](https://notes.andymatuschak.org/z4exmF6rnZTE9GjCnpFBKUT)
