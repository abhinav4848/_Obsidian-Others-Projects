I don't share my insecurities to be vulnerable for the sake of being vulnerable. I don't do it because I think this idea of vulnerability is in vogue in the professional world right now. I truly believe that it is wildly important for a leader to be open in this way. It also gives people permission to be open and not fear looking weak or being wrong.
