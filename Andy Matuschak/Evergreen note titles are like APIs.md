## Title of Atomic note

Title of Atomic note should be Clear, Concise, & Distinct (Atomic). The title for evergreen notes should be a clear title or statement. Formulating this one-liner clarifies and sharpens your thinking. And the hidden beauty is that this process naturally enforces an atomic note size‚Äîmeaning "one note = one idea".

Try your best to have only one concept within a note. If a concept has multiple notes, that's fine.
