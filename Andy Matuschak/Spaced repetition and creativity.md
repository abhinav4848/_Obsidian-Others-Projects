# Spaced repetition and creativity
[[Some kinds of creative insight depend on long-term memory]]

## Baader-Meinhof effect and SRS
Gwern suggests that one role for SRS is to stimulate the Baader-Meinhof effect.

Also references [Diaconis & Mosteller 1989](https://www.gwern.net/docs/statistics/bias/1989-diaconis.pdf#page=6) ( #external research paper), which suggests a statistical explanation for apparent coincidences; an argument like “regression to the mean” can make newly-exposed ideas suddenly appear to be everywhere.
