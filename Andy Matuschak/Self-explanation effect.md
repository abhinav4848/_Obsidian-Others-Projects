# Self-explanation effect
Students who explain what they’re learning to themselves ([[Self-explanation]]) perform better in subsequent recall, comprehension, and application tests. See main article Self-explanation for more.

Presumably related to [[Generation effect]], [[Retrieval practice]], and [[Elaborative encoding]].
